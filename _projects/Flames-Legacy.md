## Description

Flames Legacy is a 3D adventure game where you fight a huge dragon! Made for the Xcalibur'25 game jam, and awarded Best Overall.

Download <a href="https://tdavies.itch.io/flames-legacy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.

* **Tech:** ![Godot Engine](https://img.shields.io/badge/Godot-%23FFFFFF.svg?logo=godot-engine) ![GitHub](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-%23121011.svg?logo=github&logoColor=white)
* **Team:** 4 people
* **Duration:** 32 hours

## Role
* Implemented basic enemy and dragon AI
* Enhanced visuals editing sky box, and fire shaders
* Implemented character controller
